Release Cycle

Archcraft uses a rolling-release model, just like Arch Linux. That means your system stays up to date with a single command: sudo pacman -Syyu

Fresh ISOs are published four times a year — January, April, July, and October — unless a major bug or upstream issue requires an extra release.
